Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about visas, jobs, finance, and living abroad.

General Questions

What is GlobalNRI?

GlobalNRI is an information platform providing guidance on visas, jobs, cost of living, finance, and life abroad for NRIs.

Is GlobalNRI an immigration consultancy?

No. GlobalNRI provides informational content only and does not offer legal or consultancy services.

Visa & Jobs

Can I apply for a job abroad without a visa?

In most cases, you need a job offer before applying for a work visa. Some countries allow job-seeker visas.

Which country is best for IT professionals?

Countries like Netherlands, Germany, Canada, and Australia have strong demand for IT professionals.
