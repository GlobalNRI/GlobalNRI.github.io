Cost of Living in the Netherlands (2025–26)

Estimated Monthly Living Costs

Single Person: Approx. €1,700–€2,500

Family (2 adults + 2 children): Approx. €3,200–€4,500

Actual costs depend on lifestyle, city, and housing choices.

Housing

  • 1-bedroom apartment: €900–€1,500/month (varies by city)
  • 3-bedroom apartment: €1,800–€2,800/month in urban areas

Utilities & Internet

  • Electricity, water, gas: €150–€250/month
  • Internet: €35–€60/month

Food & Groceries

  • Groceries: €250–€350 per person/month
  • Restaurant meal: €15–€25 per person

Healthcare Insurance

  • Health insurance: €120–€150 per adult/month

Transportation

  • Public transport subscription: €80–€100/month
  • Biking is common and inexpensive
  • Fuel for cars: €1.90–€2.20/litre

Miscellaneous & Leisure

  • Leisure, dining out, subscriptions: €100–€200/month
  • Entertainment (cinema, events): €10–€20 per visit
