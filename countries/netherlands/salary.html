Salary, Taxes & 30% Ruling in the Netherlands

Dutch salaries are quoted as gross annual amounts. Income tax and social contributions are deducted to calculate your net pay. Expats may benefit from the 30% ruling to reduce taxable income.

Income Tax Basics

  • Progressive tax system (higher income → higher tax rate)
  • Social security contributions also deducted
  • Typical tax + contributions: 30–40% depending on salary

30% Ruling

  • Applies to highly skilled expatriates hired from abroad
  • 30% of gross salary can be paid tax-free
  • Reduces taxable income to 70% of gross salary
  • Applies up to 5 years

Other Benefits

  • Higher net income than standard tax treatment
  • Easier exchange of foreign driver’s license
  • Employer applies for the ruling with Dutch Tax Administration
