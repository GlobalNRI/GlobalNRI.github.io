Relocating to the Netherlands

Your 2026 Step-by-Step Immigration Roadmap

Phase 1: Pre-Departure (The "Minus" Months)

  • -4 Months: The Document Collection — gather original Birth and Marriage certificates; ensure passports have at least 6–12 months validity. Tip: check for fresh extracts if documents are very old.
  • -3 Months: MEA Apostille & Legalization — apostille documents with the Ministry of External Affairs (India).
  • -8 Weeks: Work Permit Application — employer submits MVV/Residence Permit request to the IND (processing times vary).
  • -3 Weeks: VFS Appointment — book and prepare to submit passport for entry visa (MVV) sticker.

Phase 2: Landing & Identity

  • Day 0: Welcome to NL — land and check into housing.
  • +1 to +3 Days: Expat Center & BSN — visit local Expat Center to receive residency sticker, register your address and apply for BSN (Citizen Service Number).
  • +3 Weeks: Banking & Insurance — once BSN arrives, open a Dutch bank account, get mandatory health insurance, and sign contracts.

Phase 3: Long-term Success

  • +8 Weeks: 30% Tax Ruling — submit application for the 30% facility if eligible.
  • +5 Years: PR & Citizenship — eligible for Permanent Residency or Naturalization; requires Inburgering (B1) exams.

Critical Pro-Tip

Never let your Residence Permit expire. Apply for renewal 3 months before expiry. A gap can reset your 5-year clock for citizenship and cause IND issues.

Work Permit — Family travelling together (summary)

  • -4 months: apply and ready birth/marriage certificates, passports (marriage certificate should be <6 months on visa day).
  • -3 months: legalize certificates (about 1 month).
  • -8 weeks: apply for work permit visa (3–6 weeks typical).
  • -2 weeks: schedule VFS appointment.
  • -1 week: submit application; passports returned in ~2 business days.
  • 0 Day: travel to NL.
  • +1 day: expat center visit, registration, BSN application.
  • +2 weeks: TB test.
  • +3 weeks: BSN arrival.
  • +6 weeks: Residence Permit arrival.
  • +10 weeks: apply for 30% ruling (timely application important for retroactivity).

Work Permit — Bachelor / travelling alone

Start at -8 weeks above; the rest is the same. An 'expedite' process may reduce processing to ~2 weeks in some cases.

Work Permit — Family joining later (not recommended)

Employee travels first. For family later: apply for and legalize certificates in India, send copies to employee in NL; employee registers marriage locally and sends confirmation; family applies for dependent visa (~-8 weeks) and follows same steps.

Tourist Visa — Sponsored

  • -6 weeks: book VFS appointment.
  • -3 weeks: obtain legalized invite letter if required.
  • -2 weeks: submit application; passport returned in ~3–5 working days.
  • 0 Day: travel to NL.

Tourist Visa — Self sponsored

Start at -6 weeks and skip the -3 week invite step.
