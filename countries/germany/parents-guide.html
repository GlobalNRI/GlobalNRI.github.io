Parenting & Childcare in Germany

Maternity/paternity leave, childcare options, parental benefits and schooling basics for expat families.

Key Topics for New Parents

  • Maternity and parental leave (Mutterschutz and Elternzeit)
  • Child benefit (Kindergeld) and parental allowances
  • Daycare options (Kita), kindergarten and school entry
  • Register child with local authorities and healthcare schedule

Practical steps

  1. Inform employer about pregnancy and check entitlements.
  2. Register birth locally and apply for Kindergeld and health coverage.
  3. Apply early for daycare places in cities where supply is tight.
