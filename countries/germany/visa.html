Germany Visa & Immigration

Overview of common immigration routes to Germany for skilled workers, students and families.

Common Visa Types

  • EU Blue Card: For highly qualified workers with a university degree and salary threshold.
  • Job Seeker Visa: 6-month visa to search for employment in Germany.
  • Work Permit / Residence Permit: Employer-sponsored work residence permit.
  • Student Visa: For international students enrolled at a recognised German institution.
  • Family Reunification: Spouse and minor children joining residents/citizens.

How to apply

Applications are submitted at German embassies/consulates for entry visas or via the local foreigners office (Ausländerbehörde) when applying from within Germany. Employers often support work permit applications.

Practical tips

  • Check salary thresholds for EU Blue Card and Blue Card validity rules.
  • Prepare degree diplomas, employment contract, passport and proof of accommodation.
  • Expect processing times of several weeks to months depending on the route.
