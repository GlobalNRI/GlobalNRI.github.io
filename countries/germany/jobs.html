Finding Work

Use German job portals (e.g., StepStone, Indeed.de), LinkedIn, company websites and recruitment agencies. Networking and referrals are important.

Salary & Contracts

Contracts typically include gross monthly salary, pension and social contributions. Ask for a written contract and clarify probation period and notice terms.
