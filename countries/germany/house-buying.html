Housing & Buying in Germany

Steps to rent or buy property, financing options, and local considerations.

Renting vs Buying

Renting is common in larger cities; buying requires larger down payments and understanding regional markets.

Buying process

  • Find a property, sign a purchase agreement, and complete notary formalities.
  • Expect additional costs: notary, land registry and transfer taxes (~3.5–6.5% depending on state).
  • Speak with local mortgage advisors for eligibility and interest rates.
