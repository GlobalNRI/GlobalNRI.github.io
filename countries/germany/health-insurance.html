Health Insurance — Germany

Most residents are covered by statutory health insurance (Gesetzliche Krankenversicherung, GKV), while higher earners may opt for private insurance.

Registering for Health Insurance

  1. Choose a statutory insurer (Krankenkasse) if eligible; employer contributions are shared.
  2. If privately insured, compare plans carefully — private premiums depend on age and health.
  3. Obtain insurance proof to register with municipality and to start work.
