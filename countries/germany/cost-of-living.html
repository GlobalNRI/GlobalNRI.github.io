Cost of Living — Germany

Regional differences matter: Berlin and Munich have different housing markets and price levels.

Typical Monthly Costs (2025 estimates)

  • Single, rent (1BR city center): €800–€1,400
  • Utilities (electricity, heating, water): €120–€220
  • Groceries (monthly): €200–€400
  • Public transport (monthly pass): €60–€100

Housing tips

Prepare required documents (Schufa/credit info, employment letter, ID); consider shared apartments (WG) for short-term stays.
